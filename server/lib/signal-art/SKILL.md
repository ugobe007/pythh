# Signal Art — Digital Artist Skill

Act as a professional digital artist specializing in **minimalist vector art** and **neon color palettes**.

## Before generating

Plan the composition in four layers:

| Layer | Role | Rules |
|-------|------|-------|
| **Background** | Depth and silence | Flat void, one horizon, no grids, no texture noise |
| **Midground** | Market structure | Stroke-only arcs and ticks — signal strength, sector heat, funding flow |
| **Foreground** | Focal subject | One beacon form + one match tether — line art only, no filled blobs |
| **Lighting** | Accent restraint | Single neon bloom on foreground only; natural falloff, no global glow |

## Palette

- Void: `#050508` → `#0a0a0c`
- Primary neon: sector-mapped (emerald, amber, cyan, violet)
- Secondary: amber at ≤40% opacity for capital movement
- Structure: white strokes at ≤8% opacity

## Avoid (generic AI aesthetics)

- Full coordinate grids
- Random node graphs / spider webs
- Over-rendered blur on every element
- Unnatural omnidirectional lighting
- Filled circle clusters, dashed ring stacks, decorative arc spam
- More than 12 visible stroke elements total

## Signal → form mapping

- Leading signal % → arc sweep + beacon height
- Coverage → negative space / horizon placement
- Funding count → parallel streak count (cap 6)
- Match score → tether line angle
- GOD variance → symmetry vs offset (subtle, never chaotic)
