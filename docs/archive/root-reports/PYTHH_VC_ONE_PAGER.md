# Pythh — One-Pager for VCs

**Tagline:** Pre-scored deal flow. Match to your thesis. Know when to pitch.

---

## What we do

Pythh scores and ranks early-stage startups (GOD score 0–100), matches them to investors by thesis/stage/sector, and surfaces **Hot Matches** — high-signal fits with momentum (funding news, traction, pedigree). VCs get a filtered pipeline instead of a raw list.

### 2. How we score (why it's a signal)

- **GOD score:** market, team, traction, product, vision + capped bonuses (momentum, investor pedigree, signals).
- **Enrichment:** RSS/news, funding mentions, investor names, sectors — so scores reflect current signals, not just a one-time form.
- **Floor:** Approved startups stay at 40+; we purge unenrichable/no-data entries so the pipeline stays clean.

So the number you see is a **current, comparable** signal, not a static vanity score.

### 3. Match quality

- **Investor–startup matching:** By sector, stage, check size, geography so you see companies that fit your mandate.
- **Hot Matches:** Ranked by fit + momentum; we can track *"X Hot Matches this week in your thesis."*
- **Conversion (when you have it):** *"% of top-ranked matches that led to a meeting / intro"* — this is the slide that makes the data credible. If you're not tracking yet, say: *"We're measuring; early signal is strong."*

### 4. Coverage and freshness

- **Coverage:** 10k+ startups, with enrichment (funding, team, traction) where we have signal.
- **Freshness:** New companies from RSS/discovery; scores and matches updated on a schedule (e.g. recalc + enrichment).
- **Uniqueness:** GOD + momentum + pedigree + news — not just Crunchbase-style static fields.
