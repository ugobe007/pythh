## 1) CORE PRINCIPLE

The homepage is a **wide capital desk** where intelligence leaks into view.

**Not:**
- A SaaS hero
- A form
- A marketing page

**Spatial philosophy:**
- Left = invocation
- Center-right = intelligence
- Bottom-left = intrigue
- Everything is asymmetrically weighted

## 3) SECTION CONTRACTS

### SECTION A — INVOCATION PANEL (LEFT-WEIGHTED)

**Purpose:** Anchor intent without turning into a form.

**Must contain:**
- Headline: "Find My Investors"
- Panel-embedded submit bar (wide, understated, instrument-like)

**Must NOT contain:**
- Big CTA button
- SaaS styling
- Boxed form feel
- Onboarding language

**Forbidden:**
- Center alignment
- Bulky rounded input
- Right-hand widget
- "Submit URL"
- "Get started"
- "Try it now"
- AI language
- How-it-works copy

---

### SECTION B — INTELLIGENCE LEAK (CENTER-RIGHT)

**Purpose:** Show the machine is alive.

**Must contain:**
- Startup name
- 3 top investors
- Signal scores
- One causal "Why" block

**Must feel like:**
- Live telemetry
- Partial revelation
- Leaked intelligence

**Forbidden:**
- Cards
- Panels
- Shadows
- "Example" labels
- "Demo" framing
- Click interactions
- Full result lists
- Expand/collapse UI

---

### SECTION C — CURIOSITY ANCHOR (BOTTOM-LEFT)

**Purpose:** Create unresolved intrigue.

**Must contain:**
- One rotating line:
  - "Capital moves before founders notice."
  - "Your narrative is legible to some funds and invisible to others."
  - "Some investors are warming up to categories you don't expect."

**Forbidden:**
- Explanation
- Instructions
- Marketing slogans
- Product narration
