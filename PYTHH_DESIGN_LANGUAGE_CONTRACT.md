## IMPLEMENTATION RULES

1. **Use "you" and "your"** — Not "the startup" or passive voice
2. **Use active voice** — "Investors see you as..." not "You're being read as..."
3. **Explain causality directly** — No metaphors, no implicit meaning
4. **Use numbers sparingly** — Match score OK, but not "signal velocity" or "convergence index"
5. **No performative language** — Delete "This is not random", "This is alive", etc.
6. **Show, don't narrate** — Let the data create wonderment, don't announce it
7. **Respect intelligence** — Founders can infer magic, don't spell it out
