# Pythh.ai Current Site Evaluation

## Brand / Identity
- Name: pythh.ai — Pythh Capital
- Tagline: "Signal Science"
- Title: "Investor signals for your raise"

## Current Design Observations

### Color Palette
- Background: Very dark navy/near-black (#0a0e14 range)
- Primary accent: Cyan/teal (light blue) — used for logo text, highlights
- Secondary accent: Green (used for positive signals, live indicators)
- Tertiary: Amber/orange (used for signal scores)
- Text: White / light gray

### Typography
- Logo: Bold, condensed font — "pythh.ai" in white, "SIGNAL SCIENCE" in cyan
- Headings: Large, bold, sans-serif — feels generic/system font
- Body: Small, low-contrast text — hard to read
- Overall: Font choices feel dated and lack personality

### Layout Issues
- Hero section is cluttered — too many elements competing for attention
- The "What founders get" section is a plain bullet list — no visual hierarchy
- The investor signals table is data-dense but lacks visual breathing room
- Three-column card section (IDENTIFY / ENGAGE / ADVANCE) is cramped
- No clear visual flow guiding the user's eye
- Footer is minimal, almost hidden

### Navigation
- Horizontal nav with: Platform, Brief, Explore, Submit, Pricing, More
- Sign in / Sign up buttons on right
- Functional but visually underwhelming

### Copy Issues
- Headline "Automate your fundraising workflow." — functional but not inspiring
- Subhead is long and dense — loses reader quickly
- "Near-term outcome: qualified investor conversations—not spray-and-pray volume." — good but buried
- "Every investor leaves a trail — portfolio moves, thesis shifts, check-size changes. We surface what they can't hide — signals in real time, scored against your startup." — this is actually GREAT copy, should be hero-level
- "What founders get" section reads like a feature checklist, not a value proposition
- Missing: social proof, testimonials, clear pricing CTA, founder-centric emotional hook

### Key Sections
1. Hero — headline + subhead + workflow steps (IDENTIFY > DEVELOP > ENGAGE > CONFIRM > ADVANCE)
2. "What Founders Get" — feature list with evidence/action/goal breakdown
3. Investor Signals Table — live data feed with sector filters
4. "Every investor leaves a trail. We follow it." — signal science explanation
5. Metrics cards: TIMING (8.7/10), FIT (94%), OPTICS (88pts)
6. The Daily Signal — newsletter/digest section
7. Footer with nav links

## What's Working
- Dark theme feels appropriate for a data/finance product
- The signal scoring concept (TIMING, FIT, OPTICS) is compelling
- Live data table is a strong proof-of-concept feature
- "We follow it." tagline is punchy

## What Needs Improvement
- Font is unappealing — needs a premium, modern typeface
- Too much light blue — needs to be reduced
- More emerald green and amber orange needed
- Hero copy needs to lead with the emotional hook
- Visual hierarchy needs significant improvement
- Sections need more whitespace and visual separation
- Missing: testimonials/social proof section
- Missing: clear "how it works" visual flow
- CTAs are weak and inconsistent
- Mobile responsiveness unclear

## Redesign Direction
- Keep: dark theme, data-forward aesthetic, signal science positioning
- Change: typography to Inter/Space Grotesk or similar premium font
- Change: color balance — less cyan, more emerald + amber
- Add: stronger hero with emotional hook
- Add: visual "how it works" section
- Add: social proof / founder testimonials
- Add: cleaner pricing CTA section
- Improve: overall visual hierarchy and breathing room
